About Me

HELLO WORLD! I'm Lily Chen.




Hello hello! I am currently a Software Development Engineer working inn Alexa AI - Natural Understanding at Amazon.

I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering and minors in Philosophy and Music Technology in Spring 2019.

Previously, I interned at Amazon as a Software Development Engineer Intern in the Alexa AI - Natural Language: NLU Engine team.


The majority of my interests lie at the intersection of hardware & software, with applications in the auditory, literary, and visual arts. You can find some of my experiments here.



In my free time, I enjoy dabbling in photography; reading mostly classic literature, poetry, and philosophy; various small arts and crafts like crocheting; and solving/playing puzzle games on my Nintendo Switch.
